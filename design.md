# Design System — Masakhe Email Automation

## Brand Colors (exact from masakhegroup.co.za audit)
- Navy dark:    #192943  (sidebar, primary surfaces)
- Navy deeper:  #0f326b  (header, hero)
- Green accent: #118849  (CTAs, active states, badges)
- Light bg:     #eef2f6  (page background)
- White:        #ffffff  (cards, content areas)
- Muted text:   #5e708d  (secondary labels)
- Body text:    #192943  (primary text)

## Typography
- Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif (ALL weights — matches site exactly)
- Sizes: 12px labels, 14px body, 16px default, 30px section heads, 48px hero
- Weights: 400 normal, 600 semibold, 700 bold, 900 display

## Layout
- Sidebar: fixed left 240px, #192943 bg
- Top bar: #0f326b, white text
- Content: #eef2f6 bg, white cards
- Buttons: #118849 bg, white text, no radius (sharp)
- Active nav: #118849 left border + bg tint

## Spacing
- Cards: 24px padding
- Sections: 24px gap
- Sidebar items: 12px vertical padding

## Email CTA Footer (every email)
- Green WhatsApp button: bg #118849, white text
- Link: https://wa.me/27695036573
- Text: "Respond via WhatsApp"
