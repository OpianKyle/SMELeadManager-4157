Email Process Flow & Follow-Up Sequence

 

Step Trigger Purpose Email Template

1 First call – No answer, no voicemail Acknowledge attempt, soft intro Connection Attempt #1

2 Second call – No answer (24h later) Create urgency, offer self-service Connection Attempt #2

3 Third call – No answer (48h later) Break-up email, keep door open Final Attempt / Hold

4 Call successful – Client requested callback later Confirm commitment, set expectation Callback Confirmation

5 Pre-onboarding – Appointment scheduled Reduce no-shows, prep client Onboarding Call Reminder

6 Client missed onboarding call (no-show) Reschedule without friction Missed Appointment

7 Post-onboarding – Call completed Summarise, next steps, welcome materials Client Onboarding Complete

 

---

 

EMAIL TEMPLATES

 

1. Connection Attempt #1 – No answer, no voicemail

 

Subject: We tried reaching you – [Your Company Name]

 

Body:

Hi [Client Name],

 

This is [Your Name] from [Your Company Name]. I just tried calling you at [time] on [phone number] to [brief reason, e.g., discuss your recent inquiry / complete your account setup].

 

No need to call back right now. You can simply reply to this email with the best number and time to reach you, or book a time directly here: [Calendar Link].

 

We’re happy to help.

 

Best regards,

[Your Name]

[Direct Line / Extension]

 

---

 

2. Connection Attempt #2 – Still no answer (24h later)

 

Subject: Still trying to reach you – [Your Company Name]

 

Body:

Hi [Client Name],

 

I tried calling again today at [time]. Since we haven’t connected yet, I want to make this easy for you.

 

Quick option: Reply with “Call me [Day] at [Time]” and I’ll adjust to your schedule.

 

Or handle this without a call: [Link to self-service action, e.g., “Complete your application here” / “Review your quote online”]

 

I’ll try one more time tomorrow. If I miss you again, I’ll assume you prefer email – just let me know.

 

Thanks,

[Your Name]

 

---

 

3. Final Attempt / Hold (48h later – soft break-up)

 

Subject: One last try – [Your Company Name]

 

Body:

Hi [Client Name],

 

I’ve tried calling a few times without reaching you. I don’t want to keep bothering you, so I’m closing your active follow-up for now.

 

If you still need help:

 

· Reply “Yes” and I’ll call you within 1 business day at a time you specify.

· Or reach us directly at [Support Email / Phone]

 

If I don’t hear from you, I’ll assume your needs have been resolved. No hard feelings.

 

Wishing you the best,

[Your Name]

 

---

 

4. Callback Confirmation (client answered and requested a future callback)

 

Subject: Confirming your callback request – [Date/Time]

 

Body:

Hi [Client Name],

 

Great speaking with you briefly! As requested, I have scheduled a callback for:

 

📅 Date: [Date]

⏰ Time: [Time] (your timezone)

📞 Caller: [Your Name] from [Your Company Name]

 

No need to prepare anything. I will call you directly at [Client’s Phone Number].

 

If that time no longer works, just click here to reschedule: [Reschedule Link]

 

I look forward to finishing our conversation.

 

Best,

[Your Name]

 

---

 

5. Onboarding Call Reminder (24h or 1h before scheduled call)

 

Subject: Reminder – Your onboarding call tomorrow at [Time]

 

Body:

Hi [Client Name],

 

This is a quick reminder about your onboarding call:

 

🗓 When: [Date] at [Time]

📍 Where: Phone call – I will call you at [Client’s Phone Number]

⏱ Duration: ~[15-20] minutes

 

What we’ll cover:

 

· Quick account setup / welcome walkthrough

· Answer any questions you have

· Next steps after this call

 

Can’t make it? Just reply “reschedule” and I’ll send you a link to pick a new time.

 

Talk soon,

[Your Name]

 

---

 

6. Missed Appointment (client did not answer at scheduled onboarding time)

 

Subject: We missed you – let’s find a new time

 

Body:

Hi [Client Name],

 

I was ready to call you at [scheduled time] for your onboarding, but we weren’t able to connect.

 

No worries – things come up.

 

Please pick a new time here: [Rescheduling Calendar Link]

 

If you’ve already completed what you needed or changed your mind, just let me know by replying “all set”. Otherwise, I’ll hold your spot for one week before closing your file.

 

Happy to help whenever you’re ready.

 

Best,

[Your Name]

 

---

 

7. Client Onboarding Complete (post-call follow-up)

 

Subject: Welcome aboard – here’s what’s next

 

Body:

Hi [Client Name],

 

Great speaking with you today! This email confirms your onboarding is complete.

 

✅ What’s been set up:

 

· [Item 1, e.g., Your account is active]

· [Item 2, e.g., First payment scheduled for Date]

 

📄 Resources for you:

 

· [Link to Welcome Guide]

· [Link to Support Portal / FAQ]

· Your dedicated support number: [Phone Number]

 

Next step from you: [One clear action, if any, e.g., “Log in and update your preferences by Friday”]

 

If anything isn’t clear, just reply to this email – I’m personally here to help for the next 48 hours.

 

Welcome again,

[Your Name]
